Welcome to the Tailors' Avenue

Your easy way to have high quality, truly bespoke outfit. We help customers to reach talented tailors from the far corners of the world and interact with them just as your local tailors. That means you have many more tailors you can choose from, and with less (sometimes much less) budget, too. And for tailors, that means your proud work can find fans all over the world.

In only 4 easy steps, you will get what maybe the best clothes you have ever owned. Send us an email now to be guided with all of the steps. Tailors' Avenue - where tailoring is no longer a luxury.

Contact us »

Step 1: Take your body measurements

Option 1: Measure your body size with the online software at http://upcload.com that accurately measures your sizes to 1.5cm

Option 2: Measure yourself with a tape measure (if you need guidance, please send us an email to get the video instructions).

Once you are done, send us an email with your measurements to place your order. Currently we take orders by emails or in person if you live in one of our supported locations (see Contact us). Later on we will have this process automatically done through the web.

Contact us »

Step 2: Fill out the questionaire with your personal styles and preferences

After receiving your email from step 1, we will contact you with a form or in person to get your personal styles and preferences. You can tell the tailor any detail you want for your clothes, too, just as you would request from your local tailors. For example, what color do you like? Do you like short or long sleeves? How about the pockets or collar? This is so that your outfit is specially made just for you. Although currently we only offer shirts, but please contact us if you need something else. We will work it out together.

Contact us »

Step 3: Check the designs recommended by the tailor

Even if you know your style, it never hurts to get advice from the experienced tailor who knows it better. After receiving your measurements and personal specifications, we work with the tailors to recommend the designs from which you can choose. You can then make your decision and let the tailor take care of the rest.

Contact us »

Step 4: Receive and pay if you like

We understand your concerns when it comes to online shopping. That is why we are offering the special "pay only if you like" policy. If you receive a shirt below your expectation, send back the shirt and give the tailor feedback. Your tailor will happily adjust and re-make the right shirt for you. Easy, trouble free, and no risk involves.
